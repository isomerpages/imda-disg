<h4><strong>AI for the Public Good — for Singapore and the World</strong></h4>
<p>Singapore’s National AI Strategy 2.0 (NAIS 2.0) sets out an ambitious,
people-centred roadmap to position Singapore as a global hub for trusted,
impactful AI. Guided by the vision of <strong>AI for the Public Good</strong>,
it aims to harness AI not just for economic transformation, but to uplift
lives—<strong>in Singapore and beyond.</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<h3><strong>What We’re Working Towards</strong></h3>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Empower_and_Excel.png">
</div>
<p>At the core of NAIS 2.0 are two guiding goals: <strong>Excellence</strong> and <strong>Empowerment</strong>.
Together, they shape a vision where Singapore not only leads in AI innovation,
but also ensures that individuals and businesses are uplifted in the process.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Innovate and Lead</strong>: Build a competitive AI ecosystem that
turns bold ideas into meaningful, <strong>trustworthy AI solutions</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Be a Global AI Destination</strong>: Make Singapore the place
to be for top AI talent tackling the world’s toughest challenges.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Widen Adoption</strong>: Make AI more accessible across sectors,
so more people and businesses can confidently harness its potential.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Empower Our Workforce</strong>: Equip people with the skills to
adapt, grow, and thrive in an AI-enabled economy.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h3><strong>How We’ll Get There</strong></h3>
<p>
<br>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">

<p>NAIS 2.0 will mobilise efforts across three core Systems—Innovation, Infrastructure,
and People—and ten strategic Enablers to drive impact.</p>
<p>From <strong>industry partnerships</strong> and cutting-edge research to <strong>open innovation platforms</strong> and <strong>global collaboration</strong>,
these efforts will support 15 priority Actions over the next 3–5 years.</p>
<p><strong>Click on the image</strong> to explore how each system and enabler
supports Singapore’s AI vision.</p>
<h3><strong>Industry at the Heart of It</strong></h3>
<p>Achieving AI excellence requires close collaboration with the ecosystem.
Through strategic partnerships, Singapore will:</p>
<p>• Co-create innovative, market-ready AI products</p>
<p>• Advance AI research at the cutting edge</p>
<p>• Build a thriving support ecosystem for AI development, deployment, and
trust-building</p>
