<h2><strong>The AI Cloud Takeoff&nbsp;&nbsp;</strong></h2>
<p><strong>An ECI pilot with Google Cloud&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<p>DISG launched a pilot with Google Cloud in Oct 2024 with 30 companies
supported by Consultant Partners Cloudmile, Searce and Kyndryl.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>The programme focused on 3 pillars:</strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Democratising knowledge through a bootcamp and 1-1 consultations</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Creating an AI Centre of Excellence Blueprint</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Building a Minimally Viable Product</p>
</li>
</ol>
